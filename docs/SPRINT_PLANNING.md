### Sprint Ceremonies

#### Daily Standup (Daily Scrum)
- **Time**: 9:00 AM
- **Duration**: 15 minutes
- **Format**: 
  - What did I do yesterday?
  - What will I do today?
  - Are there any impediments?

#### Sprint Planning
- **Duration**: 2 hours for 2-week sprint
- **Participants**: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Development Team
- **Outcomes**: 
  - Sprint Goal defined
  - Sprint Backlog created
  - Team commits to deliverables

#### Sprint Review
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Participants**: Stakeholders, Product Owner, Scrum Master, Development Team
- **Purpose**: Demonstrate completed work and gather feedback

#### Sprint Retrospective
- **Duration**: 1 hour
- **Participants**: Scrum Master, Development Team
- **Format**:
  - What went well?
  - What could be improved?
  - Action items for next sprint
